Wade Gavhan is a large village located in Parner Taluka of Ahmadnagar district, Maharashtra with total 710 families residing. The Wade Gavhan village has population of 3321 of which 1710 are males while 1611 are females as per Population Census 2011. In Wade Gavhan village population of children with age 0-6 is 396 which makes up 11.92 % of total population of village. Average Sex Ratio of Wade Gavhan village is 942 which is higher than Maharashtra state average of 929. Child Sex Ratio for the Wade Gavhan as per census is 970, higher than Maharashtra average of 894. Wade Gavhan village has higher literacy rate compared to Maharashtra. In 2011, literacy rate of Wade Gavhan village was 83.08 % compared to 82.34 % of Maharashtra. In Wade Gavhan Male literacy stands at 90.32 % while female literacy rate was 75.35 %. As per constitution of India and Panchyati Raaj Act, Wade Gavhan village is administrated by Sarpanch (Head of Village) who is elected representative of village.
